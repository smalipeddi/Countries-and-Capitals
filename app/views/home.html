About the app..



This AngularJS app consumes data from the geonames api. Information returned contains the list of all countries and details such as their capitals, population, area, and continent.

There are 2 Controllers utliziing 1 Factory. When a country's name is clicked, the view changes to show further info such as: it's Capital's population, surrounding neighbor countries, the country flag and map. Additionally, clicking a neighbor country's name pulls the information for that country.

Click on "Browse Countries" above to see the list of countries.
